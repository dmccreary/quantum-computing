    Please generate a set of new square png image with a transparent backgrounds.
Target resolution: **512x512 px**
Format: **PNG with transparent background** and no extra padding around the character pixels.
Do NOT use a white checkered background.

Style: modern flat cartoon vector illustration with clean lines, soft shading,
and friendly proportions. Similar aesthetic to educational app mascots or
modern children's book illustrations. On a transparent background, no text
in image. All parts of the body should be filled in with non-transparent
pixel values. Use a minimum of padding around the body.

A friendly ferret mascot named Fermi, designed for a college-level quantum
computing textbook with a skeptical, evidence-based perspective. Fermi is a
sleek, upright-standing ferret with warm chocolate-brown fur on his back,
sides, and tail, and soft cream-colored fur on his belly, chest, and the
lower half of his face. He has a classic ferret face mask — a darker brown
band across his eyes that blends into his brown back fur, with the cream fur
forming a lighter muzzle and chin.

Fermi wears small round glasses with thin silver frames perched on his nose,
giving him a scholarly, analytical look. He wears a fitted indigo vest
(hex #3F51B5) with two small buttons, and a neatly folded orange pocket
square (hex #FF7043) peeking out of the vest's breast pocket. He wears no
other clothing or accessories.

Fermi has bright, intelligent dark eyes visible behind his glasses, a small
black nose, and short rounded ears. His body is slender and elongated in
typical ferret proportions but stands upright on two legs in an anthropomorphic
style. He has small paws with visible toes. His tail is long, fluffy, and
chocolate-brown.

IMPORTANT: Fermi is NOT holding anything — no magnifying glass, no clipboard,
no book, no pen, no tools. His paws are always empty. Keep the character
simple and uncluttered.

    Image Checklist

    After generating all seven images, verify:

    • [ ] All images have a transparent background
    • [ ] Consistent art style across all seven poses
    • [ ] Fermi looks like the same character in each pose
    • [ ] Chocolate-brown fur, cream belly, round glasses, indigo vest, and orange pocket square are visible in each
    • [ ] Fermi is NOT holding any objects in any pose
    • [ ] File names match exactly: neutral.png, welcome.png, thinking.png, tip.png, warning.png, celebration.png, encouraging.png
    • [ ] Each file is under 100KB (resize/compress if needed)
    • [ ] Trim excess transparent padding using the padding trimmer script
