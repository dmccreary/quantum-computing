# QC Company Valuations vs. Revenue

This scatter chart plots quantum computing company valuations against
their annual revenues on logarithmic axes, alongside reference companies
from technology history. Diagonal shaded bands show normal valuation
multiples for SaaS and high-growth tech firms. The chart makes the
extreme speculation premium placed on QC companies immediately visible.

Hover over any data point to see the company name, revenue, valuation,
and enterprise-value-to-revenue multiple. Red diamond markers represent
quantum computing companies; blue circles represent reference companies
at comparable stages. The diagonal lines mark constant EV/Revenue ratios
of 10x, 50x, 100x, and 250x. Most QC companies sit deep in the red
zone (50x+ revenue), far above even the most aggressively priced
traditional tech IPOs.
