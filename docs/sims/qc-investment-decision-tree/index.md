# QC Investment Decision Tree

This MicroSim presents an interactive decision tree that walks investors
through a structured framework for evaluating quantum computing investments.
Starting from a root question about revenue growth, each branch leads through
key diagnostic questions about commercial deployment, quantum advantage,
technical milestones, and financial runway.

Click on any decision node to select a Yes or No path. The selected path
highlights while other branches fade, guiding you to one of four
color-coded outcomes: Hold (green), Reduce position (yellow), Exit (orange),
or Exit immediately (red).

Work through the tree by clicking decision nodes and selecting Yes or No.
Notice how quickly most realistic scenarios lead to "Reduce" or "Exit"
outcomes. The only path to "Hold" requires both revenue growth and
demonstrated quantum advantage -- a combination no company has yet achieved.
Use the Reset button to clear your selections and try different paths.
