# QC Investment vs. Revenue

This MicroSim visualizes the growing gap between quantum computing
investment and commercial revenue from 2015 to 2025. The orange bars
show annual investment while the green line tracks annual revenue,
making the massive disparity between the two immediately apparent.

*[View QC Investment vs. Revenue MicroSim Fullscreen](./main.html)*

The chart uses dual Y-axes so that both investment and revenue are
visible at appropriate scales. Hover over any year to see exact
dollar figures for both series. Note that cumulative investment over
this period exceeds $42 billion, while cumulative revenue totals
roughly $1.8 billion — a return on investment of approximately -96%.
