# Technology Success Indicator Analysis

This MicroSim presents a comparison matrix evaluating five technologies
against six structural indicators of commercial success. The Transistor,
Internet, GPS, and Solar Panels all show strong green checkmarks across
every indicator, reflecting their well-documented paths from early
demonstration to widespread adoption and profitability.

Quantum Computing, by contrast, shows mostly red indicators with only
partial marks for small-scale demonstration and independent verification.
The pattern matches historical technology failures, not successes.
