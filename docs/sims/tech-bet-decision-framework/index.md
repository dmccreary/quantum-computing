# Technology Bet Decision Framework

This MicroSim provides a structured scoring matrix for evaluating
whether an emerging technology is a sound investment bet. Six
criteria drawn from the analytical tools covered throughout the
course are each scored on a 1-5 scale, producing a total score
out of 30 with a color-coded verdict.

## Technology Bet Decision Framework MicroSim

[View Technology Bet Decision Framework MicroSim Fullscreen](./main.html){ .md-button .md-button--primary }

Click the score buttons (1-5) next to each criterion to rate the
technology you are evaluating. The total score and verdict update
instantly. Use the preset buttons at the bottom to load example
evaluations for the transistor (1950s) and quantum computing (2025)
to see how the framework distinguishes a strong technology bet from
a weak one.

## How to Use This Tool

1. Select a technology you want to evaluate as an investment.
2. For each of the six criteria, click the score that best matches the evidence.
3. Review the total score and verdict in the panel on the right.
4. Compare your evaluation with the built-in presets.
5. Click "Reset" to clear all scores and start a new evaluation.
