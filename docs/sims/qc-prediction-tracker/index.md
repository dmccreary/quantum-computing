# Prediction Track Record Timeline

This interactive timeline tracks major quantum computing predictions
alongside their actual outcomes. It reveals a systematic pattern of
overoptimism that spans decades, sources, and claim types.

Use the **category filters** (Failed, Disputed, Delayed, Pending)
to isolate predictions by outcome status.
Use the **source filters** (Academia, Company, Consulting) to see
which types of organizations have been most accurate — or most
overoptimistic. Click any event on the timeline to view full details
in the panel below.

The timeline makes a striking pattern visible: nearly every prediction
with a deadline that has already passed was either wrong or disputed.
The remaining "pending" predictions follow the same pattern of
ambitious claims from organizations with financial incentives to
promote quantum computing. This does not prove future predictions
will fail, but it establishes a track record that any careful
investor or policymaker should weigh heavily.
