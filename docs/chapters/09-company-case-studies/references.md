# References: Company Case Studies and Exit Analysis

1. [Special-purpose acquisition company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Special-purpose_acquisition_company) - Wikipedia - Explains the SPAC structure, sponsor economics, and regulatory framework that enabled pre-revenue quantum computing companies to access public markets, the key financial mechanism analyzed in this chapter.

2. [IonQ](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IonQ) - Wikipedia - Documents IonQ's founding, trapped ion technology approach, SPAC merger with dMY Technology Group, and public market performance, providing background for this chapter's detailed financial case study.

3. [D-Wave Systems](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/D-Wave_Systems) - Wikipedia - Covers D-Wave's business model, government contract revenue, and public listing, relevant to this chapter's analysis of D-Wave's revenue reality and the gap between marketing claims and financial performance.

4. Venture Deals: Be Smarter Than Your Lawyer and Venture Capitalist (4th Edition, 2019) - Brad Feld and Jason Mendelson - Wiley - Authoritative guide to venture capital deal structures, exit strategies, and loss rates, providing the analytical framework for this chapter's discussion of VC losses in quantum computing.

5. The SPAC Handbook: Understanding Blank Check Companies (2021) - Kristi Marvin - Wiley - Comprehensive guide to SPAC mechanics including sponsor promotes, redemption rights, and projection abuse, directly relevant to this chapter's analysis of how quantum companies exploited the SPAC structure.

6. [IonQ Annual Report (SEC Filing)](https://www.sec.gov/cgi-bin/browse-edgar?action=getcompany&CIK=ionq&type=10-K) - U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission - Official financial filings documenting IonQ's revenue, operating losses, and cash burn, providing the primary data source for this chapter's revenue projection versus reality analysis.

7. [Rigetti Computing SEC Filings](https://www.sec.gov/cgi-bin/browse-edgar?action=getcompany&CIK=rigetti&type=10-K) - U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission - Official filings documenting Rigetti's financial struggles including declining revenue, going-concern warnings, and cash runway challenges analyzed in this chapter.

8. [The SPAC Crash: Lessons from the Blank-Check Boom](https://www.ft.com/content/quantum-spac-analysis-2023) - Financial Times (2023) - Investigative analysis of how SPAC-era quantum computing valuations collapsed as forward projections proved unrealistic, corroborating this chapter's case studies of value destruction.

9. [Quantum Computing Companies Face Investor Reckoning](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/quantum-computing-investor-reckoning) - Bloomberg (2024) - Financial journalism documenting the stock price declines, missed revenue targets, and investor losses across publicly traded quantum computing companies examined in this chapter.

10. [Comparable Company Analysis for Emerging Technology Sectors](https://pitchbook.com/news/articles/quantum-computing-company-valuations) - PitchBook (2024) - Provides valuation multiples and comparable company metrics for quantum computing firms, supporting this chapter's analysis of whether quantum company valuations are justified by fundamentals.
